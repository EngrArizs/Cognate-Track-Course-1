This circuit is a 7-segment display counter using Arduino Uno and two push buttons. Signals are sent to pins 2-8 of the Arduino to control the display, lighting up different segments to form the numbers 0-9.

The two buttons serve to:
Right button increment the number
Left button decrease the number

The display is connected to the Arduino with jumper wires, and the resistors help protect the components from too much current. The breadboard is used to keep the connections organized and distribute power and ground.
to display each number on the 7 segment display. Clicking on a button changes the value of the counter and the respective number is displayed.
